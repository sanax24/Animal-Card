Blue Whale

Blue whales are MASSIVE. A blue whale's heart is as big as a mini cooper. Their tongues alone weigh as much as an elephant.

  • Scientific Name: Balaenoptera musculus
  • Average Length: female 82 ft, Male 79 ft
  • Average Lifespan: 80-110 years
  • Habitat: Ocean

A blue whale's mouth has this awesome row of plates fringed with bristles used to filter out their main source of food, Krill.
